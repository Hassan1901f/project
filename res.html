MAKE A RESERVATION

You can make a reservation at Quay online using the reservation button in the top right corner and following the prompts. Alternatively, you can call us on +61 2 9251 5600 to check availability or make a reservation. Please note that sometimes not all our tables are available online, so we do encourage you to give us a call.

If your chosen date is not available, please sign up to our waitlist. Inevitably plans change for some of our guests so tables can become available. Our reservation team can then contact you directly.
